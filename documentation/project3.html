Cybersecurity Enhancement for Financial Firm

Overview

This project focused on enhancing the cybersecurity framework of a financial firm. The primary goal was to protect sensitive financial data from unauthorized access and cyber threats, while also training employees on best security practices.

Objectives

  • Implement robust cybersecurity measures to protect financial data.
  • Configure firewalls and intrusion detection systems.
  • Train employees on cybersecurity awareness and safe practices.

Technologies Used

  • pfSense Firewall
  • Intrusion Detection System (Snort)
  • Data Encryption Tools
  • Employee Training Modules

Steps Taken

  1. Conducted a thorough security audit to identify vulnerabilities.
  2. Installed and configured pfSense firewall to monitor and block unauthorized access.
  3. Deployed an Intrusion Detection System (IDS) to detect and respond to threats.
  4. Implemented data encryption protocols for secure storage and transfer of financial information.
  5. Developed and delivered a cybersecurity training program for all employees.

Outcome

The firm now has a much stronger security posture, with significantly reduced risk of data breaches. The employees are more aware of cybersecurity threats and are actively following best practices to protect the company’s assets.
